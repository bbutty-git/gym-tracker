📋 One-time Google setup

~2 minutes. This creates a tiny private script in your Google account that writes workouts to your Sheet and Doc. No passwords or API keys ever touch this app.
  1. Create (or pick) a Google Sheet and a Google Doc for your training log, and paste their URLs into Settings.
  2. Go to script.new in your browser (opens Google Apps Script).
  3. Delete the placeholder code and paste the script below.
  4. Click Deploy → New deployment, hit the ⚙️ gear and choose Web app.
  5. Set Execute as: Me and Who has access: Anyone, then click Deploy. (“Anyone” just means the URL works without a Google login — the URL is unguessable and only writes to the Sheet/Doc you specify.)
  6. Google will ask you to authorize the script — approve it (it may warn the app is unverified: click Advanced → Go to project).
  7. Copy the Web app URL (ends in /exec) and paste it into the Apps Script bridge field. Done! 🎉
